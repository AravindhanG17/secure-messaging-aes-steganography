Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 10, 2026

1. Information We Collect

To provide our secure messaging service, we collect the following:

  • Account Data: Username and hashed passwords provided during registration.
  • Media Content: Images uploaded by users for the purpose of steganographic message delivery.
  • Message Metadata: Delivery status, timestamps, and recipient identification.

2. Use of Steganography and Encryption

Our service employs a multi-layered security approach to protect your data:

AES-256 Encryption: All hidden text messages are first encrypted using the Advanced Encryption Standard before processing.

LSB Steganography: Encrypted data is embedded into the bits of uploaded images. This ensures that the message remains invisible to the naked eye within the carrier image.

3. Data Storage and Retention

Images and encrypted messages are stored on our Secure Local Database and Protected Media Server. We retain this data only as long as your account is active. You have the right to delete any individual message or image; once deleted, the content is permanently purged from our storage and cannot be recovered.

4. Security Responsibilities

While we use industry-standard security, the "keys" required to decrypt steganographic messages are provided to the sender via QR codes. It is the user's responsibility to manage these keys. We do not store "clear-text" versions of your decryption keys in a way that allows us to access your private messages.

5. Your Rights

You may request a full export of your data or account deletion by contacting our support team. Deletion is instantaneous and covers all associated media and metadata.

6. Contact Us

For privacy concerns, please contact us at privacy@securemessaging.com.
